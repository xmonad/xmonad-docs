Usage

  1. In your xmonad.hs:
import XMonad.Prompt
import XMonad.Prompt.Man
  1. In your keybindings add something like:
    , ((modm, xK_F1), manPrompt def)

For detailed instruction on editing the key binding see the tutorial.
